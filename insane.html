If being an Arsenal fan was easy, everybody would be one! Sometimes we experience heartbreak and disappointment at a tough loss or a last minute draw when we were winning the game. But MOST times, we experience the exhilaration of snatching points when it looks like we're in for a tough night, or even a steamrolling of a notorious rival.
Below are some of my favorite moments as an Arsenal fan, one of which was a complete routing of Tottehnam 5-2 (a scoreline that we gave them twice in the same season!).

5-7 Comeback vs. Reading

We thought we were going to steamroll over Reading because they were in a lower league, but they showed up to win while we were dallying around the field! It ws an early scare, but we showed resiliency and fought back to win the match. This match also features a very young Emi Martinez who was the start goalkeeper for Argentina as they won the 2022 World Cup.

5-0 Win vs. Chelsea

I went into this match expecting to win it, but not by this scoreline. It was the heaviest defeat of Chelsea by Arsenal ever. To add insult to injury, Chelsea's rejection of Kai Havertz only came back to bite them as he score twice in this match and celebrated both goals. It is typical of players scoring against their former teams to not celebrate, but Havertz didn't hold back! This match was utter domination from beginning to end, and I loved every second.


This video of the highlights are even sweeter because they were posted by the Chelsea official YouTube channel, and they posted the EXTENDED highlights to make it hurt even more!

5-2 Win vs Tottenham

One of my favorite kind of comebacks in a match is when we are down and then comeback with multiple unanswered goals. We were down 0-2 and score 5 unanswered goals to wreck Tottenham. This game is even sweeter because Emmanuel Adebayor got his comeuppance after he left Arsenal to go to Manchester City, then to Tottenham where he utterly failed. He thought he'd get one up on us by scoring, and then we just let them have it. Karma.

3-2 Win at the Death!

One of my most recent insane moment was watching Reiss Nelson win the match against Bournemouth literally with the last kick of the ball before the end of the game. It's a special moment because Nelson was not one of our usual players. He was a squad player and desperate to be able to play in a match. He is also one of our Hale End Academy players, and it's always a special moment to see our young Gunners succeed.

Winning this match was of the utmost importance because we could NOT afford to drop any points as we were looking like serious challengers to defeat Manchester City to the title. Unfortunately, our slip up came later in the season, but at this moment, we could not be stopped!

"We Won The League at White Hart Lane"

One of my favorite moments actually occurred before I was even a fan of Arsenal. This would be one of my worst nightmares if Tottenham ever won the league title at The Emirates, so I can only imagine how badly that must've hurt the fans. They had to opportunity to stop us from winning the title by winning the match, but they could only secure a draw. They celebrated the draw as if they'd stopped us from winning, but the team gave the fans a reason to celebrate for real, with a Premier League Title!
