The Emergence of Arsene Wenger's Arsenal

Arsenal is one of the most successful clubs in English football. Arsenal have won 13 League Titles, 14 English Football Association Cups (the most in the history of the competition), and 16 English Super Cups.

A relatively unknown Frenchman named Arsene Wenger became the manager of Arsenal in 1996. Under Wenger, Arsenal saw unprecedented levels of success for the club. Wenger brought in future club legends like Dennis Bergkamp, Robert Pires, Freddie Ljungberg, and Thierry Henry. With these players, Wenger led the club to its first domestic double, winning the FA Cup and the Premier League Title back to back. Of the 14 wins of the FA Cup for Arsenal Football Club, HALF of those titles came under Arsene Wenger! What a feat!

Is Your Trophy Gold?

No other club in this league's history can ever say they've won the league without losing a single game, unless it's Arsenal. We are the only team to have a GOLD Premier League Trophy.
In the 2003/2004 season, Arsenal played 38 games, winning 26 of them, drawing 12, and losing 0! This winning team has the exclusive nickname of "The Invincibles."

Invincible Day for Arsenal fans takes place every year on May 15. Arsenal fans on social media make it a point to leave out all "L's" on their pages!
